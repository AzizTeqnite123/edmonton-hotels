Delta Edmonton Centre Suite Hotel

Enhancing the travel experience for business and leisure travelers, Delta Hotels Edmonton Centre Suites provides renovated rooms and modern amenities in the heart of downtown. Connected to Edmonton City Centre, our hotel offers easy access to the vibrant ICE District and Rogers Place. Stop by our newly renovated restaurant – District 102, downtown Edmonton’s gathering place for local-first food, game nights, and carefully crafted cocktails. Or if warm drinks are what you need join us at our Craft Cafe – serving exceptional single-origin coffee powered by Phil and Sebastian Coffee Roasters.
